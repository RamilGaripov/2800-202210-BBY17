Yoga for Complete Beginners

Summary

Yoga is not only a physical practice, but also a practice for the mind. Different types of yoga focus on different things that are important to your health. We will be focusing on "Hatha Yoga" which is focused on the body and mind.

Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga has an emphesis on having controlled breathing and good posture. You want to be able to move your body slowly into different poses while controlling your breathing.

Benefits

Those that have done Hatha Yoga may see improvements to many aspects of their life. To list a few it can reduce stress, depression symptoms. It will also improve your muscle and joint flexibility, and your core strength.
