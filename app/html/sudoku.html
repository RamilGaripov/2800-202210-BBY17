How to play If you've seen a newspaper, you've definitely seen this game. At first the game may look scary, with so many grid tiles and numbers everywhere. But don't worry! This game isn't related to math at all.

The rules are quite simple! Here they are:
Each row must contain numbers from 1-9 without any repetition.
Each column must contain numbers from 1-9 without any repetion.
